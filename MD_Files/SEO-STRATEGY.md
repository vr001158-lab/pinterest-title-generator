## ⚠️ SEO Best Practices & Pitfalls to Avoid

### ✅ DO
- Create unique, valuable content regularly
- Build natural backlinks through outreach
- Optimize for user experience (fast, mobile-friendly)
- Use keywords naturally (no keyword stuffing)
- Build authority in your niche
- Get quality, relevant backlinks

### ❌ DON'T
- Buy backlinks or participate in link schemes
- Keyword stuff (repeating keywords unnaturally)
- Create duplicate content across pages
- Cloak content (show different content to Google vs users)
- Use hidden text/links
- Create thin pages with little value
- Engage in comment spam or blog spam
